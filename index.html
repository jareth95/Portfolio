About me

I am a developer based in Essex with routes easily accessible to London. Computers have always been a big part of my life, I was brought up around a lot of computers with my dad working in IT and then owning his own computer repair business.
When I was a personal trainer I ran an online program where my clients could login and get meal/ workout plans etc.. This was using wordpress, it wasn't till my last job where I worked with developers to create algorithms for the financial market that I began delving deeper into how programming works.
I realised fairly early on into learning that this was something I wanted to pursue, so I would learn in the evenings while saving enough money to leave my position and pay for a full time programming course which led me to Just IT.
